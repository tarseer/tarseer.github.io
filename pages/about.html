About Us

The Problem

50% of those living with glaucoma aren’t aware that they have it. When left untreated, a patient can lose their sight at a rate that’s 10 times faster than the average person, and today's tool for measuring the visual field is unreliable


Our Solution

Using proprietary technology we can detect the same change using just 2 examinations. Today physicians can’t keep up with the number of tests they need to perform. By reducing the number of tests needed to measure vision loss, Tarseer can help diagnose patients 2 years earlier that current solutions. Once glaucoma is detected treatment can result in 50% decrease in the rate of damage.
